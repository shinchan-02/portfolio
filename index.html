About Me

Hey! I’m Satyam Agrawal - a Sr. SRE/DevOps Engineer who’s been wrestling cloud gremlins for 4+ years and still hasn’t lost his sense of humor (mostly).

I live where chaos meets automation. My job? Make sure your app doesn’t go down at 2 AM - and if it does, I’m the guy who fixes it before you finish your coffee. From bootstrapping self-hosted Rundeck (because who needs SaaS bills?) to upgrading MySQL 5.6 → 8.0 without breaking prod (yes, zero downtime ... nah kidding, thank you pt-online-schema-change), I turn infrastructure nightmares into boring, reliable systems.

Right now, I’m at Fleetx Technologies, keeping a fleet management platform alive for millions of vehicles. Think: 10x traffic spikes, Spot Instances, EKS auto scaling and Jenkins agents that actually work. Before that, I survived Pink Friday at Nykaa and built secure microservices at Ontic - so yeah, I’ve seen some things.

I believe in the SRE golden rule:

“If it’s not monitored, it doesn’t exist. If it’s not automated, you’re doing it wrong.”

When I’m not debugging 5xx errors or arguing with Prometheus about alert fatigue, you’ll find me:

  • Writing DevOps stories on Medium
  • Hiking in the Himalayas {In my bucket list :( } (because servers don’t follow you there)
  • Tinkering with open-source K8s tools (because why not?)
  • Explaining to my wife why “Kubernetes” is still failing

I’ve saved teams some $K/month with Spot Instances and Optimization, cut deployment time from hours to minutes, and once fixed a prod outage using only grep, tail -f and sheer willpower.
